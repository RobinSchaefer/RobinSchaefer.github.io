About me

I am a future PhD student in the Applied CL Discourse Research Lab headed by Manfred Stede. My broader interests lie in the computational processing of argumentation structure in texts. I am currently narrowing this topic in order to outline my PhD project. Due to my background in Psycholinguistics I am also working on the cognitive processing of agreement attraction effects in German using ERP data.

Additionally, I am a software developer at Debatoo.
