About me

I am PhD student in the Applied CL Discourse Research Lab headed by Manfred Stede. My broader interests lie in the computational processing of argumentation structure in texts. I am currently focusing on English and German tweets. During my Master's studies in psycholinguistics I was working on the cognitive processing of agreement attraction effects in German using ERP data.
